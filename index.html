01. About Me

Hey! My name is George and I enjoy creating applications that live either on local computers or the internet. My interest in understanding how computers work in general and how applications were created started since my early childhood. I was around eight when I had my first PC and my life changed and was fascinated (and I still am) about how such a compact device could do so many things in so little time.

Since then I would go on and off and search about how apps and games worked but with little to no commitment. I decided to get serious when I was seventeen years old when I planned (and managed in 2019) to get into Eletrical and Computer Engineering University of Peloponnese.

Three years, two COVID-19 quarantines, many ups and downs later, being disapointed with myself and the university, but having gotten some experience in application development, I decided to drop out since its main curicculum wasn't my passion afterall.

After a summer full of searching and decision making, I decided to start studying at Aegean College X Essex University since it was offering appealing courses and most of all, a degree I was very passionate about. Right now my main focus is to gain as much experience as I can get and get better at what I'm passionate about, developing functional applications that live wherever a computer or a smartphone is availabe.
